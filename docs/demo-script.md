# Demo Script

## 2-4 Minute Narrated Demo

### Opening (30 seconds)

**[Landing Page]**

"Meet CivicPulse — an AI decision copilot for faster community operations. Community teams face fragmented signals: weather alerts, citizen complaints, facility failures, all arriving through different channels. CivicPulse unifies them, prioritizes with AI, and recommends exactly what to do next."

### Scenario Load (30 seconds)

**[Click "Try Demo" → Select "Heatwave + Water Shortage"]**

"I'm loading a heatwave scenario. Watch the AI pipeline process 7 incoming signals from weather feeds, manual reports, facility status, and citizen complaints."

**[Loading animation shows: Normalizing → Enriching → Scoring → Recommending]**

"Five agents are working: intake normalizes the data, enrichment adds weather and proximity context, scoring ranks everything, and action plans are generated."

### Priority Board (45 seconds)

**[Operations Board loads with ranked incidents]**

"Here's the operations board. 7 incidents, all ranked. Notice the water supply failure is #1 — not the heatwave alert itself. Why? Because the water outage during a heatwave has compounding risk affecting 8,200 people, and the clinic nearby is already running low on supplies."

"Each card shows severity, urgency, affected population, and a composite priority score. Duplicate reports are auto-clustered — three citizen complaints about the same garbage issue become one incident."

### Incident Detail (45 seconds)

**[Click the top-priority water outage incident]**

"The detail view explains exactly why this was prioritized. The score breakdown shows urgency, severity, population impact, and compounding factors."

"AI enrichment adds: weather context about extreme heat, proximity to the affected clinic, and recommends the Water & Utilities Response Team."

### Actions & Plan (30 seconds)

**[Switch to "Actions & Plan" tab]**

"The action engine generates: deploy emergency water tankers, notify hospital administration, required resources, safety notes, and a 30-minute step-by-step plan."

### Handoff (20 seconds)

**[Click "Generate Handoff"]**

"One click generates four outputs: operator handoff summary, WhatsApp-style field message, supervisor escalation note, and a public update draft."

### Ask CivicPulse (30 seconds)

**[Navigate to Ask page → Click "Why is this ranked above the flood report?"]**

"Ask CivicPulse answers grounded questions from system state — not generic responses. The AI explains priority reasoning with evidence from the data."

### Decision Acceleration (15 seconds)

**[Back to Board → Show acceleration panel]**

"The acceleration panel shows: 95% faster priority identification, instant response summaries, 20+ issues triaged per minute. That's how CivicPulse accelerates decisions."

### Closing (10 seconds)

"CivicPulse turns scattered signals into ranked, actionable intelligence — so operators can make faster, better decisions when it matters most."
